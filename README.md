# 💳 FinSight EDIP – Enterprise Financial Intelligence Platform

## 📌 Project Overview

FinSight EDIP (Enterprise Financial Intelligence Platform) is a Business Intelligence solution developed using **SQL Server** and **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze digital payment transactions and generate actionable business insights.

The project transforms raw financial transaction data into interactive dashboards that help business executives monitor revenue, customer behavior, merchant performance, payment trends, and regional performance.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Organizations process thousands of financial transactions every day. Traditional reporting methods make it difficult to monitor business performance, identify trends, and support strategic decision-making.

FinSight EDIP solves this challenge by providing centralized Business Intelligence dashboards for real-time business analysis.
